December 30th Balance Adjustments

Date: December 30, 2024

Hello Managers,
A new balance update is now live! See the changes below.

GENERIC :

  • Escpaing clinch is now slightly harder.
  • Standing punch moves score a little less points (for victory points).
  • High Kicks have slightly lower base values.

TALENTS :

  • Rage fist: Punch damage increased from 10% to 16%, Punch combo chance increased from 10% to 12%.

NEW EFFECT :

In Addition,3 talents have their effects entirely changed :

  • Mob Brawler: Increase Critical hit change by 8% in the clinch stance. After the fighter successfully lands a critical hit in the clinch stance, reduce the opponent’s chance to successfully escape clinch by 1%. Can be stacked up to 12 times.
  • Frenzied Beating : After the fighter lands a successful hit,increase damage by 10%. Can be stacked up to 5% times resets when the fighter takes damage.
  • Staggering Strikes: Successful critical hits decrease the opponent's initative by 4¤ for 5 seconds.

Over the course of 2025, more of the least exciting talents wll get similar changes. Happy New Year, may it be filled with many medals and belts!
