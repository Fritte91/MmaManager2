Understanding About Different Disciplines Cards Are Crucial :

  • The fighter's Talents are avaible after completing chapter 14 of the main story.
  • Each fighter will individually unlock the ability to equip common, rare and epic talents respectively when reaching fighter levels 3, 5 and 7. Each fighter can equip only one talent of each rarity simultaneously
  • The first time that a fighter reaches these levels, you will get 5 talents of the corresponding rarity at random. Equipping them is free, but changing talents has a credits fee.
  • Talents are passive effects that will affect your fighter and/or their opponents during fights.
  • New Common and rare talents can be unlocked at random, respectively with common talent points (obtained through Fight Clubs) and rare talents points (obtained through the tournament).
  • New epic talents can be unlocked for credits.
  • When unlocking a new talent of a specific, rarity, you can change the equipped talent of this rarity for free for all your fighters.
